Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes! We highly recommend making a reservation, especially for dinner and weekends. You can book online through our reservations page or call us at +92 300 1234567.

Do you offer takeout and delivery?

Absolutely! You can order online through our order page for pickup or delivery. We also partner with major delivery services.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we offer vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Please inform your server of any allergies or dietary restrictions, and we'll be happy to accommodate you.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have a parking lot behind the restaurant with free parking for our guests. Street parking is also available on Main Street.

Do you host private events?

Yes! We have a private dining room and offer catering services. Visit our events page for more information or contact us to discuss your needs.

Are you family-friendly?

Absolutely! We welcome families and have a kids menu with plenty of options. High chairs and booster seats are available.
