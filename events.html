Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 4-6 weeks in advance, especially for weekends and holidays.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, a 25% deposit is required to secure your date, with the balance due 7 days before the event.

Can I bring my own decorations?

Absolutely! We welcome your personal touches. We can also provide basic decorations or recommend vendors.
