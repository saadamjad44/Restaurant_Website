Reservation Policies

  • Cancellation Policy: Please cancel at least 2 hours before your reservation time. You can call us at +92 300 1234567 or email info@cozykitchen.com
  • Late Arrivals: We'll hold your table for 15 minutes past your reservation time. After that, your table may be released to waiting guests.
  • Large Parties: For parties of 12 or more, please call us directly at +92 300 1234567 to make arrangements.
  • Special Occasions: Celebrating something special? Let us know in the special requests field and we'll do our best to make it memorable!
  • Confirmation: You'll receive a confirmation email within a few minutes. If you don't receive it, please check your spam folder or contact us.
